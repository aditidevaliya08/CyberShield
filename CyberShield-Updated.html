Module 01 · Core Concepts

The CIA Triad

The three pillars of information security that every security professional must master.

C
🔒
Confidentiality
Ensuring that information is accessible only to those authorized to have access. Prevents unauthorized disclosure of sensitive data.
Example: Encryption, access control, 2FA
I
✅
Integrity
Safeguarding the accuracy and completeness of information. Ensures data hasn't been tampered with or altered by unauthorized parties.
Example: Hashing (SHA-256), checksums, digital signatures
A
⚡
Availability
Ensuring that authorized users have access to information and systems when needed. Systems must be operational and resilient.
Example: Redundancy, DDoS protection, backups
